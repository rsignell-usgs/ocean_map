This map represents a snapshot of daily-averaged ocean surface current assembled from multiple IOOS-Partner forecast models, including: The NCOM Region 7 and the UCSC CCSNRT ROMS model. The surface current data is extracted from THREDDS Data Servers via OPeNDAP and averaged over 24 hours. The streaks are created dynamically by JavaScript running in the browser.
